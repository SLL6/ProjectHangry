Green Eggs and Ham Casserole

    • 1 Hr 35 min

Ingredients


2 lbs plum tomatoes, ripe

1 white onion, large

3 jalapeno peppers

2 fresh cilantro

1 yellow sweet pepper

1 table lime juice

Directions

1. Heat a large grill pan coated with cooking spray over medium-high heat.

2. Cut plantain in half lengthwise.

3. Cut each half crosswise into 2 pieces.

4. Spray plantain pieces with cooking spray

5. Grill 4 minutes on each side or until golden and slightly soft.

6. Sprinkle with 1/8 teaspoon salt.

7. Drizzle fish with juice

8. Sprinkle with 1/8 teaspoon salt and minced cilantro.

9. Grill 4 minutes on each side or until fish flakes easily when tested with a fork.

10. Top fish with salsa and sour cream, and serve with plantain pieces.

11. Garnish with chopped cilantro, if desired.

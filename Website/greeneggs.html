Green Eggs and Ham

45 min

Ingredients


8 eggs

1 cup frozen chopped spinach, thawed

6 ounces ham, chopped

1 cup bread cubes

1⁄2 teaspoon seasoning salt

1⁄2 cup swiss cheese, shredded


Directions

1. In a medium bowl, beat the eggs.

2. Add the spinach, ham, bread cubes, and seasoned salt. Mix well.

3. Pour into a 8x8 casserole dish that has been sprayed with no-stick spray.

4. Sprinkle with shredded swiss.

5. Bake at 375 degrees Fahrenheit for 20-30 minutes, or until set.

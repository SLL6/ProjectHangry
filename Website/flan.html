Flan

1 hr 20 min

Ingredients


1 cup white sugar

3 eggs

14 oz milk

1 tablespoon vanilla extract


Directions

1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).

2. In a saucepan over medium-low heat, melt sugar until liquefied and golden in color.

3. Carefully pour hot syrup into a 9 inch round glass baking dish, turning the dish to evenly coat the bottom and sides. Set aside.

4. Beat eggs. Beat in milk and vanilla until smooth.

5. Pour egg mixture into baking dish. Cover with aluminum foil.

6. Bake in preheated oven 60 minutes. Let cool completely.

7. To serve, carefully invert on serving plate with edges when completely cool.
